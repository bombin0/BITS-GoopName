Extended Viable Features


EVF 1 - Customer support

The first Extended viable feature we wish to implement would be that of some level of customer support. We planned on including 3 types of support, an enquiry form for users to ask questions directly from the website itself, and FAQ section in where frequent questions could be displayed with detailed answers and finally a section dedicated to all the contact information needed for users who have an issue or problem with the OES service.

Mock-ups

This first image is a representation of the enquiry form detailed in the introduction. The user would simply enter their first name and email address, along with their question and we would have some person on staff who would respond to said question by sending them an email. This form will need to be included in the overall database of the website and could relate to the user account section through the email entered.




The next image details the FAQ section, in which questions asked by users can be inputted and answered, as to reduce the number of enquiries we get on average. This section will also feature guides on how to perform some of the features we have on the OES website, such as the Augmented reality feature present on all product pages. We have also included what payment options we support on this page. The FAQ section itself will most likely expand as the website user base begins to grow and as such more and more sections and questions will be added as time goes on.




The final image details the contact information for our company, any phone numbers and business numbers that we have will be located here. All company emails and store emails will be located on this page as well. And all locations of warehouses, headquarters and physical stores will be found on this page. If the OES company expands then so will this section, adding more information as the company grows.

EVF 2 - Automatic Space Detection

The second extended viable feature we wish to implement is the automatic space detection for our augmented reality feature. Basically we want the website itself to be able to automatically alter the location of the image and rescale the product image size in accordance with some information the user inputs describing the picture they upload. say ,if a user uploads an image of their lounge room table, they could input the size of the table and the website would use that information to adjust the product image size and place it on said table. Overall this feature would make the AR feature more attractive and usable for any person who wants to use it, and would be a great addition to our website as a whole.
