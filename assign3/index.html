Aims and Goals


The aim for our online web store is to allow customers the ability to buy the right product for their space with ease. We intend to create an online store that will implement photo referencing technology to allow a seamless way to check and be sure that the customer will buy the best suited product for them.

Our first and main goal to create this online web store is to create the seamless photo referencing technology to allow customers to fit a suitable product that will one, fit the space they possess and two, too find a product that they are satisfied with. This implementation will be simple and user-friendly. This main goal will achieve our main feature of the web store to give customers the assurance of the best product.

Our second goal is to provide a service to customers that is simple and easy to understand, a website that will allow easy access to all electronics and provide the customer with the best outcome. We can achieve this with implementation of a wish list, our products page and customer account, it gives most of the work to the website to help the customer find what they need.

Our third and last goal is to make sure the website is responsive and has minimal traffic. Due to the advanced features of our website we want to make sure that on the customer end it doesn't take too long for any action, we want to make sure that all commands and functions won't create any unnecessary traffic or lag. This goal will help give more control to the user and give the best experience using our online web store.

Scope


Our working prototype includes 5 features, these are search filters, wish lists, customer accounts, product database and our photo referencing feature. The search filters will give the customer easy accessiblility to any type of product they are interested in. The wish list give the customer easy access to previous products they are interested in, this is also linked to the cusomter's account. The customer account will create the website to be more simple allowing them to add products to their wish list, checkout simplicity and create delivery easier. The product database is to allows customers a wide range of products from the cheapest to the most expensive, branded products. The photo referencing feature is the most important and allows customers to referencing their product to the space in their homes to make sure that the product they're buying is suited for them.
