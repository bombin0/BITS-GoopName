Customer Account - MVF 3 for OES website

Team member in charge of MVF - Nicholas Santosa


MVF Implementation

MVF 3 is the Customer Account. This account allows many features for the customer and allows them to continue on and add items to their wishlist. The OES website contains a signup function for any new customers and a login function in the navigation bar for easy access to any customer. Once signed up and logged in the customer is able to access the 'Account page' which displays all the relevant information about the cusomter's account.

Skeleton Website

This is the raw signup page and login page. When the user is registering a new account they have to input a Username they want, their email address, phone number and their password.

This is the raw account page. When the user logins into their account they will be redirected to their account page and it displays their details.

Furnished Website

This is the signup page. When the user is registering a new account they have to input a Username they want, their email address, phone number and their password. Once they have finished filling in all the fields it is then sent over to the users database.

This is the signup page, but if the customer doesn't fill in all the fields an error occurs and is displayed for the customer.

The login function is implemented into the navigation bar so it is accessible from anywhere on the site. Once logged in the navigation bar changes and will only display a logout button.

Once the user has signed up and logged into their account they can view their account page. This page displays all the information about their account.

The account page is only available to the customer when they have logged in, if logged out the page will tell the customer to log into their account to display any information.
